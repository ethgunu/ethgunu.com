Freqently Asked Questions

ETHGünü, is an event taking place on 13th November 2023 at Istanbul Congress Center during Devconnect Istanbul week where the Turkish community will meet Ethereum and web3, and the Turkish Ethereum community get together.
Devconnect, is an event week where the global Ethereum community comes together in different cities around the world, consisting of independent events focused on specific topics.

This year Devconnect will take place in Istanbul between 13-19 November! You can read the Devconnect announcement for more information.
